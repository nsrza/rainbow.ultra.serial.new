## PART 3: THE DMT-5 PROTOCOL

### What Is DMT-5?

Not a drug. A **reactivation protocol.**

Two forms:
1. **Short-acting dmt-5** (5-MeO-DMT derivative) - Phase 1
2. **Long-form dmt-5** (psilocybin-based, sustained) - Phase 3

### Why It Works:

Normal development suppresses apertureless cognition. Trauma survivors (orphans like Lifan) suppress deeper. dmt-5 breaks suppression, holds channels open, rewires baseline.

### The Protocol in Story:

**Phase 1 (Pre-monastery, 90 days):**
- 3x daily dosing
- Reality unstable
- Hot yoga 4-8 sessions/day
- Living with Nydan
- Accidental transmission happening

**Phase 3 (Monastery, 47 days):**
- 30g psilocybin weekly
- Niguma present for every session
- Combined with Six Frequencies practices
- Channel opening accelerated
- Day 47: Breakthrough / Timeline 4

### Key Terms:
- **Channel opening** (not "getting high")
- **Suppression breaking** (not "ego death")
- **Reactivation protocol** (not "trip")
- **Integration** (not "comedown")
- **Apertureless cognition** (what emerges)

## PART 7: THE KŌNG PROOF

Nydan's derivation. Solves the emptiness-compassion paradox.

### The Core Insight:

```
Let K = kōng perception (clarity of zero-boundary state)
Let B = ego boundary strength (sense of separate self)
Let C = compassionate action capacity

For lower intelligence (strong B):
- Can't perceive K clearly
- Must train C directly (rules, practice, effort)
- C ≠ f(K), C = learned behavior

For higher intelligence (weak B → null-self):
- Perceives K clearly (sees interconnection)
- Harming other = harming self (mechanical)
- C = f(K), compassion emerges automatically
```

### Why It Matters:

This is what the AGI needs to understand. Higher intelligence + null-self = automatic compassion. The math proves it. Lifan demonstrates it. The AGI can verify it.

- Chapter 4: Paradox setup (Rinzen presents)

- Chapter 5.5: Karmamudra breakthrough, proof derived
- Chapter 5.5: Morning presentation to group (Day 28)
