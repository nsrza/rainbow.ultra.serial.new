- Lifan isn't fighting against nature — he's completing it

## 3. THE SIX FREQUENCIES (The Emanation System)

### Orthodox View:
- Consciousness is binary: on or off, conscious or not
- Maybe "levels" of consciousness but vaguely defined
- No rigorous framework for how awareness propagates

### The Revolution (What the Monastery Teaches):

**Consciousness operates on six distinct frequencies. Emanation is frequency transmission.**

The Six Frequencies:

### FREQUENCY 1: GROUND (དངོས་པོ)
- **Quality:** Pure presence, undifferentiated awareness
- **Color:** Clear/white
- **State:** Before recognition, the field itself
- **In Combat:** The carrier wave — everything else rides on this
- **Training:** Śamatha, stillness, "just sitting"

### FREQUENCY 2: RECOGNITION (ཤེས་པ)
- **Quality:** Knowing that knows itself
- **Color:** Deep blue
- **State:** Awareness aware of awareness
- **In Combat:** Target acquisition — recognizing where to transmit
- **Training:** Vipassana, self-inquiry, "who is aware?"

### FREQUENCY 3: RESONANCE (འབྲེལ་བ)
- **Quality:** Connection, sympathy, felt-sense of other
- **Color:** Green
- **State:** Boundaries permeable, empathy as perception
- **In Combat:** Lock-on — establishing the connection before transmission
- **Training:** Tonglen, compassion practices, feeling others as self

### FREQUENCY 4: TRANSMISSION (འཕོ་བ)
- **Quality:** Active emanation, sending
- **Color:** Gold
- **State:** Consciousness broadcasting outward
- **In Combat:** The strike — sending the wake-up signal
- **Training:** Phowa, ejection practices, "giving" awareness

### FREQUENCY 5: INTEGRATION (སྦྱོར་བ)
- **Quality:** Absorption, receiving becomes being
- **Color:** Red/orange
- **State:** What was transmitted becomes permanent
- **In Combat:** Stabilization — making sure the awakening sticks
- **Training:** Tummo, inner fire, "cooking" the transmission

### FREQUENCY 6: DISSOLUTION (ཐིམ་པ)
- **Quality:** Boundaries fully gone, complete merging
- **Color:** Rainbow → clear light
- **State:** No transmitter, no receiver, only the net
- **In Combat:** The final move — total awakening or death
- **Training:** Tögal, clear light, dying practice

**How They Work Together:**

Emanation isn't one thing — it's a SEQUENCE:
1. Ground (establish presence)
2. Recognize (find the target)
3. Resonate (establish connection)
4. Transmit (send the signal)
5. Integrate (stabilize the change)
6. Dissolve (complete the awakening)

**Combat Application:**

A student learning to "bring someone online":

- Recognizes where the person is stuck (2)

- Transmits appropriate frequency (4)
- Holds until it integrates (5)

**Why It Matters for the Story:**
