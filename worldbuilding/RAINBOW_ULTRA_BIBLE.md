## THE CORE

### What This Story Is:

A science fiction transmission disguised as a thriller.

The reader thinks they're reading about AI existential risk. They ARE — but by the end, they're reading about consciousness recognizing itself. And they can't tell where one ended and the other began.

### The Experience:

**Act 1-2:** Hook them with the stakes. AI is coming. Little human brains vs superintelligence. 47 days. The fear is RATIONAL. The reader buys in completely.

**Act 3:** TELEPORT. Instant reframe. The stakes were never "will AI destroy us." The stakes were "will the net see itself?" And it just did.

**After:** The reader closes the book and looks at the world. Their world. Where AI is actually coming. And they can't unsee Indra's Net.

### The Question:

The reader finishes starving for:

**"What would the world look like if it fully awakened?"**

And then the world.
