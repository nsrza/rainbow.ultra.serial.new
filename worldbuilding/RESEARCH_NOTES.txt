84 MAHASIDDHAS - SOURCE MATERIAL

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

OVERVIEW:
- 8th-12th century CE enlightened beings in Indian subcontinent
- Achieved direct realization in a SINGLE LIFETIME
- All walks of life: kings, slaves, monks, laypeople, fishermen, prostitutes
- 4 women among the 84
- Demonstrated siddhis (supernatural powers)

KEY INSIGHT: "No matter the conditions of one's life, total realization can
be attained in a single lifetime."

NOTABLE MAHASIDDHAS:

TILOPA:
- Fisherman and sesame seed grinder
- Received direct teachings from celestial dakinis
- Many who heard his songs attained siddhis
- "Departed for enlightened realms WITHOUT LEAVING HIS PHYSICAL BODY"
- Teacher of Naropa

NAROPA:
- Underwent 12 severe trials under Tilopa to overcome ego/attachment
- Tilopa slapped him so hard he passed out
- When he woke: INSTANT equal realization to his teacher
- Mastered Mahamudra siddhi
- Brother/partner of Niguma
- Great practitioner of Samvara, Hevajra, Kalachakra

NIGUMA:
- "The Black Dakini"
- Sister or spiritual consort of Naropa (sources vary)
- Influential tantric teacher
- Appeared to Khyungpo Naljor at charnel grounds
- Founded Shangpa Kagyu lineage
- Her Six Yogas almost identical to Naropa's
- Promised to "continue to manifest in whatever form will bring benefit to beings"

VIRUPA:
- Known for miraculous powers
- STOPPED THE FLOW OF THE GANGES RIVER
- HELD THE SUN IN THE SKY
- Mastery over physical elements

NAGARJUNA:
- Founder of Madhyamaka philosophy
- Expositor of emptiness teachings

THE SIX YOGAS OF NIGUMA

Almost identical to Six Yogas of Naropa
Properly called: "Vajra Lines of the Six Dharmas"

1. TUMMO (Inner Heat)
   - Foundation practice for all others
   - Visualizing three channels (red right, white left, blue central)
   - Four chakras: crown (32 petals), throat (16), heart (6), navel (64)
   - Breathing exercises bringing winds to central channel
   - Generating inner fire at navel
   - Combined with emptiness meditation

2. ILLUSORY BODY
   - Recognizing phenomena as illusory
   - Working with subtle body

3. DREAM YOGA
   - Lucid dreaming
   - Maintaining awareness in dream states
   - **This is how Niguma can appear in dreams**

4. CLEAR LIGHT
   - Direct perception of mind's nature
   - **This = apertureless cognition**
   - Seeing reality without conceptual filters

5. PHOWA (Consciousness Transfer)
   - **THE REINCARNATION TECHNOLOGY**
   - Transferring consciousness at death
   - Controlling rebirth
   - **How tulkus maintain continuity across lifetimes**
   - **How Niguma and The Rinzen survive centuries**

6. BARDO YOGA
   - Navigating intermediate states between death and rebirth
   - Maintaining awareness through transition
