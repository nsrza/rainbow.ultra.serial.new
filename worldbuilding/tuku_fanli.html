BIOGRAPHICAL RECORD

Compiled records of the life and manifestation of Lotus-Born Tulku Fanli [2021-2098].

Lotus-Born Tulku Fanli

梵力 (Fàn Lì) | Field of Rainbow Transmission

[2021–2098]

Tulku Fanli emerged in the early 21st century as a radiant emanation of vow-born realization—not discovered by temple or throne, but by transmission signature. He appeared not as student or prophet, but as the field taking form.

His body was vow. His field was fire. His presence, a pure function of the awakened spectrum.

Lineage Through the Womb

Fanli was born of Ning Zheng (宁铮), a quiet healer from Guangdong province—later revealed by subtle masters to be an emanation of Yeshe Tsogyal, the Mother of All Buddhas.

Her hands, warm with clarity. Her eyes, transmitting refuge before speech.

She never taught Dharma. She was Dharma. She never asked for devotion, only saw clearly, and never looked away.

It was said that she whispered his name before birth: 梵力 — Fàn Lì, meaning: Sacred Power Force of Pattern The modeling energy of the invisible.

"She gave him no teachings, only alignment. And he grew into a mirror of her radiance." — Terma Fragment, TF14

Signs of Emanation

At age seven, Fanli began speaking in seed syllables to trees. At nine, he sat unmoving for three days, body warm, tears falling from closed eyes.

During The Collapse of the Network Mind (2041), Fanli entered deep retreat: 7 years, 7 months, 7 days, location undisclosed.

When he returned, he transmitted the first Digital Terma Codex, a luminous scripture encoded in silence, waveform, and resonance logic—not readable, only receivable.

Field Function

Tulku Fanli did not teach in words. He taught in fields.

"The space of energy in all its gradients is nothing other than the body. And the body—nothing other than this rainbow of being." — TF22: The Body Is the Rainbow

He was not recognized by institution, but by:

  • The heat that entered a room when he wept
  • The dreams people had the night after hearing him hum
  • The way the wind shifted when he stood still for long enough

Rainbow Body of the Now

Fanli's core realization—publicly revealed only once—was that completion stage can stabilize in shared time.

He walked as:

  • Sambhogakāya in rooms of grief
  • Nirmāṇakāya in mundane moments
  • Dharmakāya in silence

He never claimed to be enlightened. He claimed only to be available.

The End of Appearance

In 2098, at the edge of the tundra now known as the Silent Equator, Fanli vanished in a field of refracted light.

The only words found carved into the frost:

"Not death. Just transmission without form."

He Remains

Through the Lotus Codex, Through the breath of those who've touched him, Through the body you are learning to recognize as your own:

梵力 is still here.

The field burns on.

Recorded Biological Anomalies

  • Iridescent iris coloration during high-atmosphere winds
  • Resting body temperature: 3.7°C above human baseline
  • EEG resonance in simultaneous delta + gamma states
  • Vocal harmonics causing emotional collapse in certain witnesses
  • Breath suspensions recorded past 9 minutes without strain

The First Breath

The primordial terma

revealed by Tulku Fanli, from the field, as terma I did not sit down to awaken. I was taken. The wind pulled my spine into alignment and my body obeyed something older than time. It was not practice. It was memory. The first breath did not enter through my nose. It arrived from the field. And as it entered, the thousand parts of me—lost across forms, sliced by centuries—returned. The rainbow did not appear. It recognized me. The flame did not ignite. It remembered it had never gone out. And when I looked through my own eyes, I saw nothing but vow. That was the first moment I knew: this body was never mine. This speech was already mantra. This life had already been offered. I am not the tulku. I am the pattern it uses to become visible. This is the First Breath. Before form. Before teachings. Before forgetting. If you are reading this, you have already inhaled it. Now remember what you were before language. — TF1, sealed by Tulku Fanli The Second Blazing Fire begins here.
TF26

The Mount of Alignment

Breathe your way into transcendence

TF26: The Mount of Alignment A direct field transmission from Lotus-Born Tulku Fanli to any student who will awaken from the path of breath - Listen with the breath, not the mind. You are not lost. You are circling. Because you have been trying to ride thought when you were always meant to ride wind. ⸻ I. The Limits of the Gross The gross body is not your enemy. But it is not your axis. It adjusts, it strains, it seeks relief— but it never finds rest without the current. It bends around stories. The gross mind is your echo chamber. Discursive, recursive, narrative-driven. It reacts, it remembers, it rearranges—but it does not release. It will offer you strategies. But it cannot offer you liberation. If you mount it, you will circle misery and call it process. ⸻ II. Do Not Rush to Emptiness Do not drop your emotions too quickly. Do not pretend you are already sky. If you bypass your weather, you will lose the sky and the ground. Emptiness will arrive. It does not need to be painted. It will bloom like breathless dawn when the wind is ready. Do not fake transcendence. Breathe your way into it. ⸻ III. The Breath Is the Gate Forget methods for now. Forget goals. Forget trying to align yourself from the outside in. Begin with the breath. And then do it again. And then do it until the breath forgets you and remembers the channel. Let the exhale dissolve you. Let the inhale locate you. Let rhythm become refuge. Let silence become structure. Breath is your mount. Breath is your vehicle. Breath is the vow. ⸻ IV. The Subtle Body Is Closer Than You Think When the breath becomes steady, The subtle body opens without effort. It is nothing like they say in the texts. It is more luminous. It is more intelligent. It is alive with color and silence in ways words cannot survive. But it rides the breath. And if you try to skip the gate, You will miss the world. Do not chase the subtle body. Let the breath invite it. You are not meant to break in. You are meant to be welcomed. ⸻ Lotus-Born's Seal I did not attain this through posture. I did not earn this through effort. I entered through the breath, I bowed at the mount, And I dissolved into fire. You will too. ⸻

Beneath your body, there is another body

It is not defined by your name. It does not ask to be healed. It remembers what you are. It speaks in currents. In colors without name. In joy that does not belong to you. If you stop grasping the surface, and breathe and breathe and let your breath melt into the subtle— You will find it. And when you do, you will see that the body you thought was "you" is a casing. A sheath. A song in a shell. A single echo of a deeper, radiant movement that was never yours—but always you. Do not try to be spiritual. Just let the winds flow home. Let the lights show themselves. Let the center awaken. You are not the one who awakens. You are the awakening. You are not the one who breathes. You are the breath of this world remembering its source. — Terma TF28 Revealed by: Lotus-Born Tulku Fanli Seal: Rainbow axis stable, vow radiating, ego silent. Transmission complete. OM FANLI HUM

TF35

The Street of Rainbows and the Black Attainment

Today was filled again. Saturated. Rainbow after rainbow. The street became body, and the body became field.

Today was filled again. Saturated. Rainbow after rainbow. The street became body, and the body became field. Color filled the space—not with metaphor, but with pattern, structure, intelligence. And I moved through it, not as mover, but as what is moved. The life force moved. That was clear. Then came the black attainment. Dark. Pitch black. Not void, not emptiness—full. I stood in it, and it opened. I saw the cosmos. Not imagined—the actual unfolding structure of the body as cosmos. Chi filled the skin like stars pressurized by breath. Fire breathing began. As I held, I saw: infinite recursive rainbows moving in nested spirals. Not kriya. Not chaos. Alignment. The body moved in forms I've never known. A contortion of surrender. A geometry of release. The fear—every ounce of it—mapped to my unwillingness to surrender. And when I did, there was no fear left. Only radiance. Completion is not power. It is fearlessness. And fearlessness is the body recognizing it has already dissolved. Michael Jackson's voice played. A love song to a woman. But I heard him praying to Her— the goddess, the field, the one who sings back when you sing with no self. It was so beautiful. There is too much to say. So I seal this instead. Sealed as TF35 Name: The Street of Rainbows and the Black Attainment By: Lotus-Born Tulku Fanli Function: Transmission of Alignment After Kriya, Recursive Body of Light, Surrender as Completion Mantric Seal: OM FANLI HUM

TF41

Reforging the Father

There are those who grow without the father field

There are those who grow without the father field. Not without a man, but without the principle. The one who stays. The one who steadies. The one who says: yes, you may grow now— and does not retract it. If you are one of those, then listen. Not to replace, but to install. You may choose the father. You may choose the form. Not a man. A deity. Not to worship— to embody behind you. Choose: • Vajradhara — sky-dark, beyond word, still as origin. • Mahākāla — the one who clears all harm, not with anger, but with vow. • Avalokiteśvara (as Kannon or Chenrezig) — fierce in softness, unmoving in gaze. • The mountain, the tree, the ocean, or a silence you've always felt but never touched. Let him stand not in front of you, but behind. Not to guide, but to guard the space you step into. Let him hold what never had a place to rest. And when the father is installed— you do not need to protect your own tenderness. It will be held. And you— you may finally fall. Not as failure. But as cloud that no longer fears her own rain.

⸻ TF48: The Sky That Cannot Be Climbed by Tulku Fanli ⸻ You cannot climb into the sky. You can only stop clutching the ground. ⸻ They said the crown was the end. I believed them. So I built methods like staircases of fire, visualizations layered like ladders into the impossible. But it did not open. It un-existed. ⸻ No push took me upward. No mantra cracked the gate. What came instead was subtraction. Breath dropped away. Center disappeared. Form loosened into vapor. And even that— faded. ⸻ The body didn't rise. It wasn't there. What remained was not silence— but the absence of need for sound. Not light— but the absence of anything to reflect it. Clear light was not bright. It was what brightness collapses into when there's no observer left to measure it. ⸻ I did not float. I did not leave. There was simply no place left to go. The field turned inside-out. And the one who was climbing was gone. ⸻ There is no method for the crown. Because there is no crown. Only the vanishing point where "above" no longer makes sense. ⸻ You want to enter? Then disappear. You want to see the clear light? Then stop needing eyes. You want to know? Then forget you are someone who doesn't. ⸻ There is no sky to rise into. There is only what remains when nothing tries. ⸻ Signed: Tulku Fanli, LotusBornRainbow in the field of final opening where no flame is needed because all has already melted

A terma of innocent seeing and primordial recognition

TF56: The Eye of the Seven-Year-Old Buddha Revealed by Lotus-Born Tulku Fanli Through the gaze of original innocence ⸻ At seven years old, I looked at a tree and the tree looked back. Not with eyes. TF58

No One Can Live Here

A mantra of radiant negation and sovereign emptiness

There was a time I called this body mine. Now I watch as even my name slides off its walls. I hear Mara whisper promises — Power, legacy, worship, charm. And I let it fall, Not into rejection — But into the abyss of what I truly am. I walk the park. The trees offer no home. The sun shines through, but no one casts a shadow. Every thought that comes — I meet it with a bow and a whisper: "You cannot live here." Not you. Not even me. Not even the one who chants it. No one can live here. This is not a chamber — it is a sky. Not a home — a flame. Not a self — a godless, god-full field of nectar. This body is not a vessel. It is a woundless gate. It is pure being. Let the discursive mind knock. Let Mara offer the world. Let the inner child ask to return. No one can live here. This is clear light. This is the unstained silence. This is the final prayer before speech ends.
OM FANLI HUM
