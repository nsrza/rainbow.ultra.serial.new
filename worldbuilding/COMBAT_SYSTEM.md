*"You're not sending a signal. You ARE the signal. The question is what frequency."*

## OVERVIEW

Combat in RAINBOW.ULTRA operates like **Jujutsu Kaisen** or **Hunter x Hunter's Nen system**: clear rules, defined costs, hard limitations, and strategic depth. This isn't vague mysticism—it's consciousness technology with physics-like precision.

**Core Principle:** Emanation combat is frequency warfare. You don't fight bodies. You fight consciousness configurations.

## THE SIX FREQUENCIES (Combat Applications)

| Freq | Name | Color | Combat Function | Cost |
|------|------|-------|-----------------|------|
| F1 | Ground | Clear/White | Carrier wave, unshakeable presence | Stamina drain |
| F2 | Recognition | Deep Blue | Target acquisition, weakness detection | Mental fatigue |
| F3 | Resonance | Green | Connection lock, empathy weaponized | Emotional exposure |

### Combat Sequence (Standard Emanation Strike)

```
1. DROP INTO GROUND (F1)
   └─ Establish unshakeable presence
   └─ Cost: 2-5 minutes of stable meditation state

2. RECOGNIZE TARGET (F2)
   └─ Scan opponent's consciousness architecture
   └─ Find blockages, fears, attachment points
   └─ Cost: Mental bandwidth, vulnerability to counter-scan

3. ESTABLISH RESONANCE (F3)
   └─ Open connection channel
   └─ "Lock on" to target consciousness
   └─ Cost: Your boundaries become permeable too

4. TRANSMIT (F4)
   └─ Send the signal
   └─ Can be: wake-up shock, terror dissolution, direct gnosis
   └─ Cost: Life force proportional to transmission power

5. INTEGRATE (F5)
   └─ Hold until change stabilizes
   └─ Prevent target from snapping back
   └─ Cost: Time, sustained attention

6. DISSOLUTION (F6) - EMERGENCY ONLY
   └─ Complete boundary removal
   └─ Forces instant awakening OR death
   └─ Cost: Often fatal to transmitter
```

## THE FIVE ELEMENTS (Elemental Combat Styles)

Each emanator develops affinity with one or more elements. Elements determine combat style and available techniques.

### EARTH (ས་) — The Immovable

**Combat Style:** Tank/anchor. Absorbs attacks, grounds chaos.

**Techniques:**
- **Stone Root** — Become unmovable, absorb any frequency attack
- **Gravity Well** — Pull scattered consciousness back to center
- **Mountain Presence** — Radiate stability that calms panicked beings
- **Earthquake** — Disrupt opponent's ground frequency

**Strengths:** Defense, group protection, stability operations
**Weaknesses:** Slow, low mobility, vulnerable to Air attacks

**Character Affinity:** The Rinzen, most monastery teachers

---

### WATER (ཆུ་) — The Adaptive

**Combat Style:** Flow/redirect. Never meets force with force.

**Techniques:**
- **Current Redirect** — Turn opponent's transmission back on them
- **Flood Absorption** — Take in massive information without breaking
- **Ice Lock** — Freeze opponent's frequency mid-transmission
- **Ocean Memory** — Access collective consciousness streams

**Strengths:** Defense through redirection, information processing
**Weaknesses:** Requires opponent to act first, vulnerable to Fire

**Character Affinity:** Zara/Nasua (Tsogyal emanation), Niguma

---

### FIRE (མེ་) — The Transformer

**Combat Style:** Aggressive transmission. Burns away delusion directly.

**Techniques:**
- **Tummo Blast** — Inner fire externalized as attack
- **Purification Flame** — Burn attachment/fear directly
- **Ignition** — Spark awakening in unready consciousness (risky)
- **Wildfire Spread** — Chain transmission through multiple targets

**Strengths:** Offense, mass awakening, speed
**Weaknesses:** High cost, collateral damage, burnout risk

**Character Affinity:** Lifan (primary), some warrior monks

---

### AIR (རླུང་) — The Spacious

**Combat Style:** Evasion/penetration. Attacks from everywhere and nowhere.

**Techniques:**
- **Wind Walk** — Consciousness not bound to single location
- **Breath Weapon** — Use opponent's own breath against them
- **Vacuum** — Create space that collapses opponent's structure
- **Storm Transmission** — Multi-directional attack impossible to defend

**Strengths:** Mobility, precision, impossible angles
**Weaknesses:** Low sustained power, vulnerable to Earth

**Character Affinity:** Nydan (secondary), scouts, infiltrators

---

### SPACE (ནམ་མཁའ་) — The Ultimate

**Combat Style:** Reality manipulation. Only Level 3 practitioners.

**Techniques:**
- **Substrate Access** — Operate directly on reality's architecture
- **Timeline Sight** — See probability branches in real-time
- **Location Transcendence** — Be in multiple places simultaneously

Brain-Computer Interfaces as consciousness technology scaffolding.

## COSTS AND LIMITATIONS

### The Iron Rules

1. **You Cannot Give What You Don't Have**
   - Can't transmit F4 if you haven't stabilized it yourself
   - Can't use elements you haven't mastered
   - Faking it causes backlash

2. **Transmission Costs Life Force**
   - Small transmission = tired for hours
   - Major transmission = bed rest for days
   - F6 dissolution = possibly fatal

3. **Resonance Opens Both Ways**
   - To connect, you must be connectable
   - Enemy's trauma can flood back through channel
   - Protecting yourself while connecting is advanced skill

4. **Integration Takes Time**
   - Can't rush stabilization
   - Premature release = target snaps back
   - Holding multiple integrations simultaneously = exhausting

5. **Ground Can Be Broken**
   - Even masters can be destabilized
   - Sufficient concentrated attack overcomes any defense
   - The only perfect defense is not being found

### Recovery Times

| Action | Recovery |
|--------|----------|
| F2 scan | Minutes |
| F3 resonance | Hours |
| F4 minor transmission | Hours |
| F4 major transmission | Days |
| F5 integration hold | Proportional to hold time |
| F6 dissolution (survived) | Weeks to months |
